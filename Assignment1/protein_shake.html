Yakisoba Chicken and Rice

Ingredients

  • 60g dry oats
  • 500mL almond milk
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 2tbsp peanut butter

Preparation

  1. Blend dry oats in a blender until powderized finely.
  2. Add almond milk into blended oats and blend until combined.
  3. Add one frozen banana, one scoop of protein powder and 2tbsp of peanut butter and top off with almond milk.
  4. Blend until completely combined.
