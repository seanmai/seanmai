Yakisoba Chicken and Rice

Ingredients

  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 4 cups brocollo florettes
  • 1 green onion
  • 2lb chicken thigh
  • 6tbsp teriyaki sauce
  • 1tbsp olive oil

Preparation

  1. Mince the garlic and thinly slice the onion.
  2. In a medium-hot pan, heat up the olive oil.
  3. Stir fry the chicken until crispy and brown.
  4. Add the garlic and onion to the pan and stir fry until translucent and cooked through.
  5. Add the brocolli and continue to stir fry until cooked through.
  6. Combine the teriyaki sauce and stir until the ingredients are evenly coated.
  7. Serve over steamed rice.
  8. Freshly chop the green onion and add to the top of the chicken and rice.
