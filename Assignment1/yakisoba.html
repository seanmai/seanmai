Yakisoba Chicken and Rice

Ingredients

  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 carrot
  • 5 mushrooms
  • 4 cabbage leaves
  • 1 green onion
  • 1lb pork belly or flank steak
  • 6tbsp yakisoba sauce
  • 1tbsp olive oil

Preparation

  1. Slice onion, carrots, mushroom and cabbage into strips.
  2. Bring a pot to the boil and boil the noodles for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Strain and cool the noodles.
  4. In a medium-hot pan, heat up the olive oil.
  5. Fry the meat until no longer pink and remove.
  6. Stir fry the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  7. Add the carrot, mushrooms and cabbage.
  8. Combine the noodles and meat and mix in the yakisoba sauce.
  9. Freshly chop the green onion and add to the top of the yakisoba.
